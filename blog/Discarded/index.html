Design Principle - Playful Simplification

Incorporating as many reasonable functions as possible within a single widget (multiple events on an element in coding parlance) realizes simplification of visual design without sacrificing user experience. Instead, users may be attached to the playfulness generated by the multitasking setting, and thus adapt themselves to the new behavioral instructions. Functions, accordingly, need to be declared with consideration of inclusiveness and expansiveness.
